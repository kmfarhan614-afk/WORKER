About Us

Welcome to New Trimmer Hair Salon & Beautician Center, your premier destination for professional hair and beauty services.

Established in 1998, we have been serving our community with excellence and dedication for over two decades. Our salon is staffed with highly trained professionals who are passionate about delivering exceptional results.

Located in Din Bahar, near Haji Abad, Charsadda Road, we pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of hygiene, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

Whether you're looking for a simple haircut or a complete beauty transformation, our expert team is ready to make you look and feel your best.
